Health benefits of going vegan

Penny Peasgood, manager of the Yogan Shop says: “The idea of our family business sprung from our conviction of the many health benefits of eating vegan. I have struggled with my weight all my life and tried all sorts of diets with no long-term success.
However, after a few months of vegan food, all those extra pounds had just melted. Also, I used to suffer from chronic fatigue but suddenly, I started to burst with energy. Needless to say, that I was delighted.
At first, my family thought I was being fussy as we all had been meat-eaters before. Seeing my elevated mood and betterment, my mum followed in my footsteps and her type-2 diabetes was a thing of the past within two years.
My dad took a bit more time to give it a go but he has not regretted it! His arthritis got so much better that he enjoys playing football again."
